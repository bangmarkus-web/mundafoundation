Munda Foundation supports children and young people through art, music and community. We develop local activities together with partners in Sierra Leone and Norway.

Who we are

Munda is a common family name in Sierra Leone and means “ours” in Mende. The name honors our uncle Munda Rogers, who dedicated his life to helping others. We continue his legacy through practical work and projects that offer safe and creative spaces for children and youth.

  • Music education and instrument library
  • Art workshops and creative gatherings
  • Local partnerships with schools and volunteers
  • Donations of equipment and capacity building
